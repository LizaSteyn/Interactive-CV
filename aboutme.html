Who am I?

On a personal level I am an introverted, almost 30-year-old, with a small, close-knit group of friends and a love for learning and travelling. My hobbies include reading, playing video games, solving puzzles and being creative. I am the youngest or second youngest (biologically and non-biologically) of the family. I was born in Pretoria and spend the first 5 years of my life living in Rooihuiskraal, after which we moved to small town Brits. I completed my primary education at Generaal Hendrik Schoeman primary school and my high school education at Brits High School. Through out my live my mother has been a great source of inspiration and she has taught me values to live by that will help me throughout my live.
I am currently at the beginning of what I consider my career marathon. During this marathon, a few wrong turns and tumbles were taken, but my path has been righted and I learned valuable lessons from my mistakes that will most definitely help me in my future career path. In short, I am a hard working and diligent person who has valuable life experience that will assist me in achieving all set forth goals and expectations.
